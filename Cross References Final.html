Cross References Feature

Now Available on Kedus Bible  

Dear Kedus Bible User,

We are excited to announce a powerful new feature in the Kedus Bible app—Cross References!


This feature enriches your Bible study experience by seamlessly linking related passages, allowing you to explore the the Scripture in depth.


How to Use Cross References:

  • Discover how a verse connects to others, helping you understand the broader context of Scripture.


  • Navigate between verses that are thematically or contextually linked, allowing for a more comprehensive Bible study.


  • Explore how different passages of the Bible reference and illuminate one another, revealing connections that may not have been immediately obvious



 
 

How to Use Cross References:

  • Tap on a verse: Any verse with a cross reference will display a small ref icon.


  • Tap the icon: View a list of related passages.


  • Explore further: Tap on any related passage to jump directly to it.


Open any passage in the Kedus Bible, and you'll find cross references highlighted for you to explore. We hope this new feature enhances your study and helps you discover even more within the Scriptures.
